Idea

The (unitary) geometric nerve is a natural nerve operation on bicategories. It is a functor from BiCat to sSet. This is also sometimes called the Duskin nerve. The notion is implicit in work by R. Street (1987). The direct approach was used by Duskin in work at about the same time, as explained in both articles. (Duskinâ€™s article directly on the idea was published in 2002.)

The construction, thus, yields a functor:

Special cases of the construction relate to earlier constructions relating to the homotopy coherent nerve, see below for more detail.
