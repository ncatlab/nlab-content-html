Definition

An H *H^*-algebra is a ℂ \mathbb{C} -algebra AA that is simultaneously a Hilbert space (A,⟨⋅,⋅⟩ A)(A, \langle \cdot, \cdot \rangle_A) with a compatible star-algebra structure, namely with an anti-linear involution †:A→A\dagger \colon A \to A such that

⟨ab,c⟩ A=⟨b,a †c⟩ A=⟨a,cb †⟩ A \langle a b, c \rangle_A = \langle b, a^{\dagger} c \rangle_A = \langle a , c b^{\dagger} \rangle_A

for all a,b,c∈Aa,b,c \in A.
