Idea

One says ordinary cohomology for the cohomology theory represented by the Eilenberg-MacLane spectrum K(ùî∏)K(\mathbb{A}) for some abelian group AA.

This is to distinguish from more general notions of cohomology, such as generalized (Eilenberg-Steenrod) cohomology and nonabelian cohomology.
