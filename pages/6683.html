For k=2k = 2 the supersymmetry of the ABJM model increases to ùí©=8\mathcal{N} = 8. For k=2k = 2 and N=2N = 2 the ABJM model reduces to the BLG model (ABJM 08, section 2.6).

Due to the matter coupling, the ABJM model is no longer a topological field theory as pure Chern-Simons is, but it is still a conformal field theory. As such it is thought to correspond under AdS-CFT duality to M-theory on AdS4 √ó\times S7/‚Ñ§/k\mathbb{Z}/k (see also MFFGME 09).

Notice that the worldvolume SU(N)SU(N) gauge group enhancement at an ‚Ñ§ k\mathbb{Z}_k-ADE singularity is akin to the gauge symmetry enhancement of the effective field theory for M-theory on G2-manifolds at the same kind of singularities (see at M-theory on G2-manifolds ‚Äì Nonabelian gauge groups).
