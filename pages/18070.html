Idea

In mathematics typically by default the term ‚Äúconfiguration space‚Äù of a topological space XX refers to the topological space of pairwise distinct points in XX, also called Fadell's configuration space, for emphasis.

In principle many other kinds of configurations and the spaces these form may be referred to by ‚Äúconfiguration space‚Äù, notably in physics the usage is in a broader sense, see at configuration space (physics).
