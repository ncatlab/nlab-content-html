Idea

What is called the Œ≤\beta-Œ≥\gamma system is a 2-dimensional quantum field theory defined on Riemann surfaces XX whose fields are pairs consisting of a (0,0)(0,0)-form and a (1,0)(1,0)-form and whose equations of motion demand these fields to be holomorphic differential forms.

The name results from the traditional symbols for these fields, which are
