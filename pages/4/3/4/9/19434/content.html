Idea

The concept of a Yoneda structure provides in a general 2-categorical setting the axiomatic description of the formal properties of the usual presheaf construction and Yoneda embedding of (locally) small categories. The size issues arising in that context are absorbed directly into the structure via a class of (locally) â€œsmallâ€ maps.
