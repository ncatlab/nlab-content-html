Kellogg Stelle is a professor of theoretical physics at Imperial College London.

Selected writings

On black M2-brane-solutions to D=11 supergravity:

  • Mike Duff, Kellogg Stelle, Multi-membrane solutions of D=11D = 11 supergravity, Phys. Lett. B 253, 113 (1991) (spire:299386, doi:10.1016/0370-2693(91)91371-2)

On the Hopf-Wess-Zumino term of the M5-brane:

  • Jussi Kalkkinen, Kellogg Stelle, Section 3.2 of: Large Gauge Transformations in M-theory, J. Geom. Phys. 48 (2003) 100-132 (arXiv:hep-th/0212081)

On higher curvature corrections to D=11 supergravity and M-theory on G2-manifolds:

  • H. Lu, Christopher Pope, Kellogg Stelle, Paul Townsend, Supersymmetric Deformations of G 2G_2 Manifolds from Higher-Order Corrections to String and M-Theory, JHEP 0410:019, 2004 (arXiv:hep-th/0312002)

  • H. Lu, Christopher Pope, Kellogg Stelle, Paul Townsend, String and M-theory Deformations of Manifolds with Special Holonomy, JHEP 0507:075, 2005 (arXiv:hep-th/0410176)

On M-theory on Calabi-Yau 5-folds and supersymmetric quantum mechanics:
