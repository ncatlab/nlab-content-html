Speculations: limits and contractible models ?

In sPhi\sPhi the notions of homotopy and limit are closely related: the same construction in sPhi\sPhi describes both picking a homotopy contracting a topological space (via singular complexes) and taking a limit of a filter on a topological space, and applies to an arbitrary morphism in sPhi\sPhi. One may perhaps hope that this construction can somehow be used to define a useful notion of a {\em contractible} (generalised topological space associated with a) {\em model}, %M •M_\bullet, or that the notion of a {\em limit of a combinatorial structure on a model}, say a tree as in NTP, is useful (i.e.~taking a limit of the morphism associated with a combinatorial structure on a model, such as those arising in the lifting properties describing NTP).
