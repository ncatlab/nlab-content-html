Horizontal Composition

Definition

Given

  • kk be a separably closed field,

  • κ\kappa be an uncountable strong limit cardinal

write

  • Sch /Spec(k) ≤κSch^{\leq \kappa}_{/Spec(k)} for the category of κ\kappa-small schemes over Spec(k)Spec(k),

    equivalently this is the category of κ\kappa-small profinite sets,

for the canonical geometric morphism.

Remark

The base topos in (1) is equivalently called the category of κ\kappa-condensed sets.

The following seems to be claimed:

Proposition

The geometric morphism (1) has the following properties:

  • i *i^\ast is fully faithful;

  • i *i_\ast is a left adjoint

and

  • i *i^\ast is a right adjoint.

The first two claims are fairly standard, the last one would make the topos of pro-étale schemes be locally connected and hence almost cohesive over κ\kappa-condensed sets (by Rem. ).
