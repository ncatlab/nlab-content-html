Proposition

For n≥1n \geq 1, the homotopy groups of complex projective space ℂP n\mathbb{C}P^n are the integers in degree 2, the homotopy groups of the 2n+1-sphere in degrees ≥2n+1\geq 2n+1 and trivial otherwise:

(1)π k(ℂP n)={* | k=0 1 | k=1 ℤ | k=2 ℤ | k=2n+1 π k(S 2n+1) | k≥2n+1 0 | otherwise \pi_k \big( \mathbb{C}P^n \big) \;=\; \left\{ \array{ \ast &\vert& k = 0 \\ 1 &\vert& k = 1 \\ \mathbb{Z} &\vert& k = 2 \\ \mathbb{Z} &\vert& k = 2n + 1 \\ \pi_k \big( S^{2n+1} \big) &\vert& k \geq 2n+1 \\ 0 &\vert& otherwise } \right.
