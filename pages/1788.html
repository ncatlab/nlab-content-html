Example

Let EE be a multiplicative Whitehead-generalized cohomology theory. Then for all n∈ℕn \in \mathbb{N} there is an unstable cohomology operation

[Ω ∞Σ nE⟶(−) k ∪Ω ∞Σ knE]∈E˜ 2k(Ω ∞Σ nE) \big[ \Omega^\infty \Sigma^n E \overset{ (-)^{k_\cup} }{\longrightarrow} \Omega^\infty \Sigma^{k n} E \big] \;\;\in\;\; \widetilde E^{ 2 k } \big( \Omega^\infty \Sigma^n E \big)
