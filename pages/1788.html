Proposition

Let

  • H\mathbf{H} be an ∞ \infty -topos;

  • G∈Groups(H)G \,\in\, Groups(\mathbf{H}) a group object ( ∞ \infty -group);

  • G↷X∈AAct(H)G \curvearrowright X \,\in\, A Act(\mathbf{H}) an ∞ \infty -action of GG on XX.

If this is an ∞\infty-free action in that all its higher shear maps are (-1)-truncated

then the homotopy quotient

X⫽G=lim⟶[n]∈Δ opX×G × n∈H X \!\sslash\! G \,=\, \underset{\underset{[n] \in \Delta^{op}}{\longrightarrow}}{\lim} X \times G^{\times_n} \;\;\; \in \; \mathbf{H}

is equivalent to the plain quotient, namely the coequalizer

τ 0(X)/τ 0(G)=coeq(τ 0(X)×τ 0(G)⇉−−−τ 0(X))∈H 0 \tau_0(X) / \tau_0(G) \,=\, coeq \big( \tau_0(X) \times \tau_0(G) \overset{\phantom{---}}{\rightrightarrows} \tau_0(X) \big) \;\;\; \in \; \mathbf{H}_0

of 0-truncated objects

H 0⊥↪i 0←τ 0H \mathbf{H}_{0} \underoverset {\underset{i_0}{\hookrightarrow}} {\overset{\tau_0}{\leftarrow}} {\;\; \bot \;\;} \mathbf{H}

in that

X⫽G≃i 0(τ 0(X)/τ 0(G))∈H 0↪i 0H. X \!\sslash\! G \;\; \simeq \;\; i_0\big( \tau_0(X)/\tau_0(G) \big) \;\;\; \in \; \mathbf{H}_0 \xhookrightarrow{i_0} \mathbf{H} \,.

In particular, if both XX and GG are already 0-truncated, then the action of GG on XX is ∞\infty-free iff it is free in the ordinary sense, and then the homotopy quotient coincides with their ordinary quotient.
