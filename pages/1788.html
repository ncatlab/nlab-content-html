2-Functor

Idea

A 22-functor F:‚Ñ≠‚ÜíùîáF:\mathfrak{C}\to\mathfrak{D} is a mapping between 22-categories that respects the composition of 1-cells/2-cells and identity 1-cells/2-cells in an appropriate sense. At the 2-categorical level there are several possible versions of this notion one might want depending on the given setting, all of which collapse to the standard definition of a functor between categories when considered on 22-categories with discrete hom-categories (viewed as 11-categories). The least restrictive of these is a lax functor, and the strictest is (appropriately) called a strict 2-functor.
