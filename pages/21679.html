Preliminaries

Definition

(nilpotent and finite rational homotopy types)

Write

(1)Ho(SimplicialSets Qu) ≥1,nil ℚ,fin ℚ↪AAAHo(SimplicialSets Qu) Ho \big( SimplicialSets_{Qu} \big)^{\mathbb{Q}, fin_{\mathbb{Q}}}_{\geq 1, nil} \overset{ \phantom{AAA} }{\hookrightarrow} Ho \big( SimplicialSets_{Qu} \big)

for the full subcategory of the classical homotopy category (homotopy category of the classical model structure on simplicial sets) on those homotopy types XX which are

  • rational

  • and in addition

    • connected: π 0(X)=*\pi_0(X) = \ast

    • nilpotent: π 1(X)\pi_1(X) is a nilpotent group

    • rational finite type: dim ℚ(H n(X;,ℚ))<∞dim_{\mathbb{Q}}\big( H^n(X;,\mathbb{Q}) \big) \lt \infty for all n∈ℕn \in \mathbb{N}.

Similarly, write

(2)Ho(DiffGradedCommAlgebras ℚ ≥0) fin ≥1↪AAAHo(DiffGradedCommAlgebras ℚ ≥0) Ho \big( DiffGradedCommAlgebras^{\geq 0}_{\mathbb{Q}} \big)_{fin}^{\geq 1} \overset{ \phantom{AAA} }{\hookrightarrow} Ho \big( DiffGradedCommAlgebras^{\geq 0}_{\mathbb{Q}} \big)

for the full subcategory of the homotopy category of the projective model structure on connective dgc-algebras on those dgc-algebras AA which are

  • connected: H 0(A)≃ℚH^0(A) \simeq \mathbb{Q}

  • finite type: dim ℚ(H n(A))<∞dim_{\mathbb{Q}}\big( H^n(A) \big) \lt \infty for all n∈ℕn \in \mathbb{N}.

(Bousfield-Gugenheim 76, 9.2)

Statement

Proposition

(fundamental theorem of dg-algebraic rational homotopy theory)

The derived adjunction

Ho((DiffGradedCommAlgebras k ≥0) proj op)⊥⟶ℝexp⟵𝕃Ω PLdR •Ho(HoSimplicialSets Qu) Ho \left( \big( DiffGradedCommAlgebras^{\geq 0}_{k} \big)^{op}_{proj} \right) \underoverset { \underset {\;\;\; \mathbb{R} exp \;\;\;} {\longrightarrow} } { \overset {\;\;\; \mathbb{L} \Omega^\bullet_{PLdR}\;\;\;} {\longleftarrow} } {\bot} Ho \big( HoSimplicialSets_{Qu} \big)

of the Quillen adjunction between simplicial sets and connective dgc-algebras (whose left adjoint is the PL de Rham complex-functor) restricts to an equivalence of categories between the full subcategories of nilpotent and finite rational homotopy types from Def. :

Ho((DiffGradedCommAlgebras k ≥0) proj op) fin ≥1≃⟶ℝexp⟵𝕃Ω PLdR •Ho(HoSimplicialSets Qu) ≥1,nil ℚ,fin ℚ Ho \left( \big( DiffGradedCommAlgebras^{\geq 0}_{k} \big)^{op}_{proj} \right)^{\geq 1}_{fin} \underoverset { \underset {\;\;\; \mathbb{R} exp \;\;\;} {\longrightarrow} } { \overset {\;\;\; \mathbb{L} \Omega^\bullet_{PLdR}\;\;\;} {\longleftarrow} } {\simeq} Ho \big( HoSimplicialSets_{Qu} \big)^{\mathbb{Q}, fin_{\mathbb{Q}}}_{\geq 1, nil}

(Bousfield-Gugenheim 76, Theorem 9.4)
