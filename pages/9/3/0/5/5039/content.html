Models in Modal Logics

To give the standard (geometric) semantics of modal logics, one needs models and these will be discussed here and in the companion algebraic models for modal logics.

This outlines the basics of the semantics often called Kripke semantics of these logics. We concentrate on modal languages with unary modalities and therefore on relational structures with binary relations in them only. This is largely for the sake of keeping the exposition fairly straightforward and simple. This means the frames used will be â€˜Kripke framesâ€™.
