Idea

In joint generalization of the cobordism cohomology theories MU and MFr of closed UU-manifolds and of FrFr-manifolds, respectively, a (U,fr)(U,fr)-manifold (Conner-Floyd 66, Section 16, Conner-Smith 69, Sections 6, 13) is a compact manifold with boundary equipped with unitary group-tangential structure on its stable tangent bundle and equipped with a trivialization (stable framing) of that over the boundary.

The corresponding bordism classes form a bordism ring denoted Œ© ‚Ä¢ U,fr\Omega^{\mathrm{U},fr}_\bullet.

Relation to Todd classes and the e-invariant

Proposition

(e-invariant is Todd class of cobounding (U,fr)-manifold)

Evaluation of the Todd class on (U,fr)(U,fr)-manifolds yields rational numbers which are integers on actual UU-manifolds.
