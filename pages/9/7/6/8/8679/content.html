Idea

The Turaev-Viro model, or more precisely Turaev-Viro-Barrett-Westbury model, is a 3d TQFT construction induced from a spherical category/fusion category ùíû\mathcal{C}.

If ùíû\mathcal{C} is moreover a modular tensor category then there exists also the Reshetikhin-Turaev construction 3d TQFT. In this case the Turaev-Viro model is something like the ‚Äúnorm square‚Äù of the Reshetikhin-Turaev construction on ùíû\mathcal{C}.

For GG a finite group and ùíû=Vect G\mathcal{C} = Vect_G the category of GG-graded vector spaces the Turaev-Viro model describes the GG-Dijkgraaf-Witten theory, also the Levin-Wen model.

See for instance the introduction of (Kirillov-Balsam 10) for a review.

Properties

As an extended TQFT

The Turaev-Viro model has been constructed as a 3-2-1 extended TQFT in (Kirillov-Balsam 10, Balsam 10a, Balsam 10b).

Related but different is the construction of fully extended 3d TQFT from fusion categories via the cobordism theorem, see at fusion category ‚Äì Relation to extended 3d TQFT for more on this.
