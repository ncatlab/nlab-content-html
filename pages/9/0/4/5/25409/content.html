Idea

The knots-quivers correspondence [KRSS19] is kind of correspondence between knots and quivers conjectured from geometric engineering via intersecting D-brane models of both knot invariants and quiver gauge theory.

Related concepts

  • McKay correspondence

  • AGT correspondence

  • 3d-3d correspondence
