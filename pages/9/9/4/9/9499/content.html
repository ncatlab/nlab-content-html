Idea

A generalization of the notion of foliation of a smooth manifold from manifolds to Lie algebroids.

One of several equivalent definitions of a (regular) foliation of a smooth manifold is

Definition

A regular foliation of a smooth manifold XX is a wide sub-Lie algebroid of its tangent Lie algebroid, hence a Lie algebroid ùí´\mathcal{P} over XX with injective anchor map
