More abstractly, given that √©tale morphisms of schemes may be characterized as modal morphisms with respect to an infinitesimal shape modality, one can consider √©tale toposes in every context of differential cohesion. This we discuss in

√âtale topos of a scheme

An √©tale topos is the sheaf topos over an √©tale site, hence over a site whose ‚Äúopen subsets‚Äù are √©tale morphisms into the base space. The intrinsic cohomology of an √©tale (‚àû,1)-topos is √©tale cohomology.
