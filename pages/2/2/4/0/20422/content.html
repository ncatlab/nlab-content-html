Idea

Given a vertex operator algebra ùí±\mathcal{V} (VOA) or super vertex operator algebra (sVOA), or more generally a full 2d CFT (of which the VOA is the local and chiral data) or 2d SCFT (hence a ‚Äú2-spectral triple‚Äù) one may ask (as for any object in any category) for its automorphisms, hence the homomorphisms

In perturbative string vacua and Connes-Lott models

If ùí±\mathcal{V} is a direct summand of a 2d CFT/2-spectral triple encoding a perturbative string theory vacuum, such as, typically, a rational 2d CFT such as a Gepner model encoding a ‚Äúnon-geometric‚Äù KK-compactification, then the automorphisms of ùí±\mathcal{V} are the formal duals to symmetries of that KK-compactification-fiber space (see the references below)
