Idea

The Euler class œá\chi (or ee) is a characteristic class of the special orthogonal group, hence of oriented real vector bundles.

The Euler class of the tangent bundle of a smooth manifold XX, evaluated on its fundamental class, is its Euler characteristic œá[X]\chi[X].

Properties

Cup square

For EE a vector bundle of even rank rank(E)=2krank(E) = 2 k, the cup product of the Euler class with itself equals the kkth Pontryagin class

(1)œá(E)‚å£œá(E)=p k(E). \chi(E) \smile \chi(E) \;=\; p_k(E) \,.

(e.g. Walschap 04, Section 6.3, p. 187)

When the Euler class is represented by the Euler form of a connection ‚àá\nabla on EE, which then is fiber-wise proportional to the Pfaffian of the curvature form F ‚àáF_\nabla of ‚àá\nabla, the relation (1) corresponds to the fact that the product of a Pfaffian with itself is the determinant: (Pf(F ‚àá)) 2=det(F ‚àá)\big( Pf(F_\nabla) \big)^2 = det(F_\nabla).

Whitney sum formula

Proposition

(Euler class takes Whitney sum to cup product)

The Euler class of the Whitney sum of two oriented real vector bundles to the cup product of the separate Euler classes:

œá(E‚äïF)=œá(E)‚å£œá(F). \chi( E \oplus F ) \;=\; \chi(E) \smile \chi(F) \,.

(e.g. Walschap 04, Section 6.4)
