Idea

A lax extranatural transformation is to a lax transformation as an extranatural transformation is to a natural transformation.

Definition

Let 𝒜\mathcal{A}, ℬ\mathcal{B}, 𝒞\mathcal{C}, and 𝒟\mathcal{D} be bicategories and P:𝒜×ℬ op×ℬ→𝒟P\colon\mathcal{A}\times\mathcal{B}^\mathsf{op}\times\mathcal{B}\to\mathcal{D} and Q:𝒜×𝒞 op×𝒞→𝒟Q\colon\mathcal{A}\times\mathcal{C}^\mathsf{op}\times\mathcal{C}\to\mathcal{D} be pseudofunctors (we are going to use the invertibility of their lax functoriality constraints below). The following is [Corner 2017, Definition 2.2], but laxified:

A lax extranatural transformation P⇒•QP\overset{\bullet}{\Rightarrow}Q from PP to QQ consists of
