Idea

A free operad is free on a collection of operations.

Given a collection kk-ary operations-to-be for each kâˆˆâ„•k \in \mathbb{N}, the free operad on this collection has as nn-ary operations the collection of all trees with nn leaves equipped with a labelling of each vertex vv with a kk-ary operation, for kk the incoming edges to vv.
