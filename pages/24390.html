 Overview

Strings 2022 is a theoretical physics conference held in Vienna, Austria between 18 July and 22 July 2022. This conference consists of a variety of subjects at the frontier of theoretical physics, such as conformal field theory, holography, black hole physics, string theory, and other topics in quantum field theory and quantum gravity. The website for this conference could be found here.
