Equivariant cell structure on 2-tori

The following shows for the 2D crystallographic groups (wallpaper groups) SS

the G G -CW complex structure on the resulting G-tori T 2≔ℝ 2/ ℓℤ 2T^2 \coloneqq \mathbb{R}^2/_{\ell}\mathbb{Z}^2 (graphics from SS25).

The point groups GG arising are the cyclic groups ℤ /1=\mathbb{Z}_{/1} = 1 1 , ℤ /2 \mathbb{Z}_{/2} , ℤ /3 \mathbb{Z}_{/3} , ℤ /4 \mathbb{Z}_{/4} , ℤ /6\mathbb{Z}_{/6} and the dihedral groups Dih 1Dih_1, Dih 2Dih_2, Dih 3Dih_3, Dih 4Dih_4, and Dih 6Dih_6 (making 9 distinct abstract point groups GG, due to the isomorphism ℤ /2≃Dih 1\mathbb{Z}_{/2} \simeq Dih_1, but several come with distinct group actions on ℝ 2\mathbb{R}^2).
