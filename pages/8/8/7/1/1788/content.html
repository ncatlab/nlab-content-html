Theorem

(Pasting Law for Pullbacks in a Cube)
Suppose we have a commutative cube in a category:


  • Assume the front and bottom faces are pullback squares. Then the following are equivalent:

    1. The total rectangle from U′→V′U'\to V' to X→YX\to Y is a pullback.

    2. The top and back faces are pullback squares.

    3. The top face or the back face is a pullback square.

  • Assume the top and back faces are pushout squares. Then the following are equivalent:

    1. The total rectangle from U′→V′U'\to V' to X→YX\to Y is a pushout.

    2. The front and bottom faces are pushout squares.

    3. The front face or the bottom face is a pushout square.
