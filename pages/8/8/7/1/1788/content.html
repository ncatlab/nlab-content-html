Dimensional reduction of 5d Maxwell-Chern-Simons to 3D Chern-Simons

0 =F 2 (XX)F 1 (Xl) 0 =F 2 (XX)F 1 (Xv) 0 =F 2 (XX)F 0 (lv)+F 1 (Xl)F 1 (Xv). \begin{aligned} 0 & = F_2^{(X X)} F_1^{(X l)} \\ 0 & = F_2^{(X X)} F_1^{(X v)} \\ 0 & = F_2^{(X X)} F_0^{(l v)} + F_1^{(X l)} F_1^{(X v)} \mathrlap{\,.} \end{aligned}
