integer Heisenberg group={(a,b,[n])∈ℤ×ℤ×ℤ 2|k|, (a,b,[n])⋅(a′,b′,[n′])=(a+a′,b+b′,[n+n′+a⋅b′−a′⋅b])} \text{integer Heisenberg group} \;=\; \Bigg\{ \begin{array}{l} \big(a, b, [n]\big) \,\in\, \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}_{2\vert k\vert} \,, \\ \big( a, b, [n] \big) \cdot \big( a', b', [n'] \big) \;=\; \big( a + a' ,\, b + b' ,\, [n + n' + {\color{red} a \cdot b' - a' \cdot b}] \big) \end{array} \!\!\!\!\!\!\!\! \Bigg\}

Given k∈ℤk \in \mathbb{Z}, for brevity we will now write ℤ 2^\widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} specifically for the integer Heisenberg group

ℤ 2^≔{(a,b,[n])∈ℤ×ℤ×ℤ k} \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} \;\coloneqq\; \Big\{ (a,b,[n]) \,\in\, \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z} \times \mathbb{Z}_k \Big\}

We clearly have an automorphism group

Sp(2,ℤ) → Aut(ℤ 2^) g longmapsto (a→,b→,[n])↦(g(a→),g(b→),[n]) \begin{array}{ccc} Sp(2,\mathbb{Z}) &\xrightarrow{}& Aut\big( \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} \big) \\ g &\longmapsto& \big( \vec a,\, \vec b,\, [n] \big) \mapsto \big( g(\vec a),\, g(\vec b),\, [n] \big) \end{array}

The non-trivial irrep of the integer Heisenberg group extension ℤ 2k↪ℤ 2^↠ℤ 2\mathbb{Z}_{2 k} \hookrightarrow \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} \twoheadrightarrow \mathbb{Z}^2

ℤ 2^ →W Aut(ℋ 1) \begin{array}{ccc} \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} &\xrightarrow{ W }& \mathrm{Aut}(\mathscr{H}_1) \end{array}

may be given by

ℋ 1≃ℂ(|[n]⟩|[n]∈ℤ 2k) \mathscr{H}_1 \;\simeq\; \mathbb{C}\Big( \left\vert [n] \right\rangle \,\Big\vert\, [n] \in \mathbb{Z}_{2k} \Big)

W(n a,n b,0)|[n]|:=e 2πin a|[n+n b]|. W(n_a, n_b, 0) \left\vert [n] \right\vert \;:=\; e^{ 2 \pi \mathrm{i} n_a } \left\vert [n + n_b] \right\vert \,.

Consider the group automorphism

ℤ 2^ →S ℤ 2^ W a ↦ W b −1 W b ↦ W a ζ ↦ ζ \begin{array}{ccl} \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} &\xrightarrow{ \; S \; }& \widehat{\mathbb{Z}^2} \\ W_a &\mapsto& W_b^{-1} \\ W_b &\mapsto& W_a \\ \zeta &\mapsto& \zeta \end{array}

We claim that this is compensated by the following linear map

ℋ 1 →S ℋ 1 |[n]⟩ ↦ ∑ [n^]exp(2πin^nk)|[n^]⟩ \begin{array}{ccl} \mathscr{H}_1 &\xrightarrow{ S }& \mathscr{H}_1 \\ \left\vert [n] \right\rangle &\mapsto& \sum_{ [ \widehat{n} ] } \exp\big( 2 \pi \mathrm{i}\tfrac{\widehat{n} n}{k} \big) \left\vert [\widehat{n}] \right\rangle \end{array}
