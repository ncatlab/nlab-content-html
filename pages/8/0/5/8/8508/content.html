Idea

A super Riemann surface is the analog of a Riemann surface in supergeometry.

Examples

A Riemann surface with spin structure that is also a branched cover of ℂP (1|1)\mathbb{C}P^{(1 \vert 1)} is canonically endowed with the structure of a super Riemann surface with Ramond punctures? (Donagi-Witten 15). This implies that adinkras encode certain very special super Riemann surfaces Doran & Iga & Landweber & Mendez-Diez 13.
