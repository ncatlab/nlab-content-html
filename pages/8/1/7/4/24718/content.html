Idea

A wreath is a generalisation of a distributive law between two monads in a 2-category. While a distributive law in a 2-category KK can be seen as an object of Mnd(Mnd(K))Mnd(Mnd(K)), a wreath can be seen as an object of EM(EM(K))EM(EM(K)), where EMEM denotes the completion of a 2-category under Eilenberg‚ÄìMoore objects.
