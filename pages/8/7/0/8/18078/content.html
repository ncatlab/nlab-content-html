Definition

Given a 2-category KK equipped with proarrows according to Def. , we can construct a double category having the same objects as KK, whose vertical 1-cells are the arrows, whose horizontal 1-cells are the proarrows, and whose squares are the 2-cells H→J(g,f)H \to J(g,f).

Remark

Dually, the 2-cells can be also represented as string diagrams (Myers 2016).
