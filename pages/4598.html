Idea

The Pontryagin classes are characteristic classes on the classifying space ‚Ñ¨O(n)\mathcal{B}O(n) of the orthogonal group and, by pullback, on the base of any bundle with structural group the orthogonal group. The latter is where they were originally defined.

The analogs for the unitary group are the Chern classes.

The universal Pontryagin characteristic classes P kP_k on the classifying space BO(n)B O(n) are, up to a sign, the pullbacks of the Chern classes c 2kc_{2k} along the complexification inclusion
