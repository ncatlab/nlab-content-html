Properties

Universal orientation

For an abelian compact Lie group GG, equivariant complex cobordism theory MU GMU_G is an equivariant complex oriented cohomology theory (Greenlees 01, Sec. 13).

Much as in the non-equivariant case (see at universal complex orientation on MU), MU GMU_G is universal in that there is a bijection between equivariant complex orientations (in degree 2) on some cohomology theory E GE_G and homotopy ring homomorphisms of GG-spectra MU G→E GMU_G \to E_G (Cole-Greenlees-Kriz 02, Theorem 1.2).

For the analogous statement on the equivariant Lazard ring see Greenlees 01a, Greenlees 01, Theorem 13.1, Cole-Greenlees-Kriz 02, Theorem 1.3.
