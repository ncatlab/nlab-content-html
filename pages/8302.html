Idea

Ordinary homology is generalized homology with respect to an Eilenberg-MacLane spectrum HAH A, and often understood over H‚Ñ§H \mathbb{Z}.

Equivalently this is computed by singular homology with coefficients in AA.

Properties

Relation to homotopy groups

Ordinary homology with coefficients in ‚Ñ§\mathbb{Z} of a topological space XX serves to approximate the homotopy groups of XX.

See for instance at Hurewicz theorem
