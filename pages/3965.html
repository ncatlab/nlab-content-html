Idea

The purpose of this page is to explain - using appropriate mathematical terminology - Dirac‚Äôs theory of coupling particles with spin to a Yang-Mills gauge field.

We proceed in three steps: first we recall relevant facts about the gauge field itself, then we discuss charged particles in gauge fields, and finally we add spin.
