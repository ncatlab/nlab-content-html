Idea

In perturbative quantum field theory the St√ºckelberg-Petermann renormalization group (St√ºckelberg-Petermann 53) is the (original) incarnation of the renormalization group in the perspective of causal perturbation theory: It is a group of formal deformations of interaction Lagrangian densities which is such that any two choices of renormalized perturbative S-matrices differ by precomposition with a unique group element. This is the statement of the main theorem of perturbative renormalization.
