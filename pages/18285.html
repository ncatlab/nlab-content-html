A source of examples

Roughly speaking, an ‚àû\infty-cosmos is a simplicially enriched category of fibrations and fibrant objects.

Let ‚Ñ≥\mathcal{M} be a model category that is enriched over the Joyal model structure on simplicial sets. Then the full subcategory of fibrant objects defines an ‚àû\infty-cosmos. If, as is frequently the case in practice, every fibrant object is cofibrant, then this subcategory defines an ‚àû\infty-cosmos with all objects cofibrant, which admits a simpler axiomatization.
