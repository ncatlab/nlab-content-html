Idea

Examples

A stable (‚àû,1)-category is naturally enriched in the (‚àû,1)-category of spectra.

More generally, for RR an E-‚àû ring then an RR-linear (‚àû,1)-category is naturally enriched in RR-‚àû-modules. (This includes the previous case for RR the sphere spectrum.)

A closed monoidal (‚àû,1)-category is naturally enriched over itself.

(Gepner-Haugseng 13)
