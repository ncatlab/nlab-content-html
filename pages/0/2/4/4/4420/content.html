Idea

The spectrum of an abelian category (or Rosenberg‚Äôs spectrum) is a set attached to a svelte (equivalent to small) abelian category generalizing the left spectrum of a noncommutative ring. It can be equipped with natural Zariski topology and a stack of local abelian categories, similar to the picture of locally ringed spaces. It has been invented by A. L. Rosenberg in 1980-s.
