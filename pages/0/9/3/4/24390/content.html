The meeting

  • Strings 2022

    Vienna, Austria.

    18-22 July (2022)

    indico.cern:1085701

was an installment of the annual string theory-conference, on the first two days with some emphasis on quantum gravity-topics via holography/AdS-CFT duality (such as black hole entropy in string theory).
