Definition

A fork

is split if it can be embedded into a diagram

in which se=id As e = id_A, tg=id Bt g = id_B and tf=est f = e s.

Of course, ff and gg can be interchanged in the definition.

Properties

Every split fork is an absolute equalizer, but not conversely.
