Idea

A symmetric monoidal functor is a functor F:C‚ÜíDF : C \to D between symmetric monoidal categories that is a monoidal functor which respects the symmetry on both sides.

As long as it goes between symmetric monoidal categories a symmetric monoidal functor is the same as a braided monoidal functor.
