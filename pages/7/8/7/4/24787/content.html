Definition

An enhanced factorisation system on a 2-category is a factorisation system on a 2-category such that, for any invertible 2-cell α:te⇒ms\alpha : t e \Rightarrow m s

there is a unique pair of a 1-cell r:B→Cr : B \to C and invertible 2-cell β:t⇒mr\beta : t \Rightarrow m r such that re=sr e = s and βe=α\beta e = \alpha.
