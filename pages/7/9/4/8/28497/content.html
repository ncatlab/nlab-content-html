Idea

An exhaustive list of notations, and denotions for theoretical mathematics.

Glossary

  • ∧\wedge The null-ordered logic conjunction, or AND operator. For formula, ϕ,ψ\phi,\psi,

Theorem

ϕ∧ψ=¬(¬ϕ∨¬ψ)\phi \wedge \psi = \neg (\neg \phi \vee \neg \psi)

  • ∧ A\wedge _A The meet operator in the meet-semilattice AA.

  • ∨\vee The null-ordered logic inclusive dysjunction, or OR operator.

  • ∨ A\vee _A The join operator in the join-semilattice AA.

  • ⊻\veebar The null-ordered logic exclusive dysjunction, or XOR operator.

  • ⇒\Rightarrow The null-ordered logic implication, conditional, or implies operator. For formula, ϕ,ψ\phi, \psi,

Theorem

ϕ⇒ψ=¬ϕ∨ψ\phi \Rightarrow \psi = \neg \phi \vee \psi

  • ⇔\Leftrightarrow The null-ordered logic equivalence, biconditional, or equals operator. For formula ϕ,ψ\phi, \psi,

Theorem

ϕ⇔ψ=ϕ⇒ψ∧ψ⇒ϕ\phi \Leftrightarrow \psi = \phi \Rightarrow \psi \wedge \psi \Rightarrow \phi

  • ∀\forall The first-order logic universal quantifier. For a predicate formula ϕ\phi, ∀αϕ(α)=¬∃α¬ϕ(α)\forall \alpha \phi (\alpha) = \neg \exists \alpha \neg \phi (\alpha).

  • ∃\exists The first-order logic existential quantifier.

  • ∃!\exists ! The first-order logic uniqueness quantifier.

  • ↑ A\uparrow _A (({α\alpha})) The principal up-set, coinitial/final segment generated by the singleton subset {α\alpha} in the partial order, or join-semilattice AA.

  • ↑ A(α)\uparrow _A (\alpha) The up-set, or coinitial/final segment generated by the subset α\alpha in the partial order, or join-semilattice AA.

  • {x|ϕ(x;y i)x|\phi(x;y_i)} The ϕ\phi-definable predicate class. For set-variable xx, and class-variable AA,

Theorem

∀x(x∈A⇔ϕ(x)\forall x (x\in A \Leftrightarrow \phi (x)

  • 00 The first limit-ordinal in the Von Neumann ordinals, i.e. ∅. The initial category.

  • 0 A0_A The bottom element, or the zero of complete partial order, or bound meet-semilattice AA.

  • 11 The first successor-ordinal in the Von Neumann ordinals, i.e. ∅. The terminal category.

  • 1 A1_A The top element, or unit of the complete partial order, or bound meet-semilattice AA.

  • A iA_i The predicate class of i -cells in n-category A.

  • Arr(A),A 1Arr(A), A_1 The predicate class of morphisms, or 1-cells in category AA.

  • Fun A(B,C),Hom A(B,C)Fun_A(B,C), Hom_A(B,C) The hom-set, or hom-object called the functor category of functors from domain BB, to codomain CC in category AA.

  • Hom A(x,y),hom(x,y),A(x,y)Hom_A(x,y), hom(x,y), A(x,y) The hom-set, or hom-object of morphisms from domain xx, and codomain yy in category AA.

  • Nat A(f,g)Nat _A(f,g), Hom A(f,g)Hom_A(f,g) The hom-set or hom-object of natural transformations from domain f, and codomain g in functor category AA.

  • Ob(A),A 0Ob(A), A_0 The predicate class of objects, or 0-cells in category AA.

  • ZFC The standard first-order foundational material pure axiomatic set theoretical logic with Axiom of Choice.
