Idea

In higher geometric prequantization (see there for more details for the moment) the notion of prequantum circle bundle is refined to that of a prequantum circle n-bundle with connection for all n∈ℕn \in \mathbb{N}.

Associated higher prequantum line bundle

Given an E-infinity ring EE and an infinity-representation

ρ:B n−1U(1)→GL 1(E) \rho \colon \mathbf{B}^{n-1} U(1) \to GL_1(E)

we have the associated infinity-bundle

X→B nU(1) conn→EMod X \to \mathbf{B}^n U(1)_{conn} \to E Mod

This is the higher analog of the prequantum line bundle, the higher prequantum line bundle.

See at motivic quantization for more on this.
