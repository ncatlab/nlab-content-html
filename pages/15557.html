Idea

The Weil zeta function is a zeta function for arithmetic varieties over finite fields which, under the function field analogy, is analogous to the Dedekind zeta function for number fields KK (and hence of the Riemann zeta function, for K=‚ÑöK = \mathbb{Q}).

The (proof of the) Weil conjectures concerns the properties of the Weil zeta function.
