Ideas

Mayer-Vietoris sequence is the term for the fiber sequence ‚Äì or often for the corresponding long exact sequence of homotopy groups ‚Äì induced from an (‚àû,1)-pullback (or for a homotopy pullback presenting it).

(Co)Homology of a cover

A special case of the general Mayer-Vietoris sequence, corollary ‚Äì which historically was the first case considered ‚Äì applies to the cohomology/homology of a topological space XX equipped with an open cover {U 1,U 2‚ÜíX}\{U_1, U_2 \to X\}.
