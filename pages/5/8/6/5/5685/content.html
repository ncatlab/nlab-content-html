Selected writings

Introducing the notion that came to be known as Isbell duality:

  • John Isbell, Structure of categories, Bulletin of the American Mathematical Society 72 (1966), 619-655. [euclid:1183528163, ams:1966-72-04/S0002-9904-1966-11541-0]

  • John Isbell, Normal completions of categories, Reports of the Midwest Category Seminar, 47, Springer (1967) 110-155 [doi:10.1007/BFb0074302]

On large cocompleteness:

  • John Isbell. Small subcategories and completeness. Mathematical systems theory 2 1 (1968) 27-50 [doi:10.1007/BF01691344]

On the theory of locales (and introducing that term):

  • John R. Isbell, Atomless Parts of Spaces, Mathematica Scandinavica 31 (1972) 5–32 [doi:10.7146/math.scand.a-11409]
