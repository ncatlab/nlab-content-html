Idea

There is supposed to be a (p=3)(p=3)-brane in 6-dimensional super-spacetime given by the Green-Schwarz action functional induced by the exceptional super Lie algebra (3+2)(3+2)-cocycle on ùî∞ùî¶ùî∞ùî¨(5,1)\mathfrak{siso}(5,1) (Hughes-Liu-Polchinski 86).

This is thought to be the intersection locus of two M5-branes (Papadopoulos & Townsend 1996, Tseytlin 1996, Howe, Lambert & West 1998, p. 2, Kachru, Oz & Yin 1998).

Since this brane has codimension 2, it is a defect brane.
