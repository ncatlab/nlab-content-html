Selected writings

Early discussion of the D'Auria-Fré formulation of supergravity:

  • Riccardo D'Auria, Pietro Fré, Paul Townsend, Invariance of actions, rheonomy and the new minimal N=1N=1 supergravity in the group manifold approach, Ann. Phys. 155 (1984) 423-446 [cds:143990, doi:10.1016/0003-4916(84)90007-1, pdf]

On D=6 supergravity formulated on superspace:

  • Moustafa A. Awada, Paul Townsend, Germán Sierra: Six-dimensional simple and extended chiral supergravity in superspace, Class. Quantum Grav. 2 (1985) L85 [doi:10.1088/0264-9381/2/4/005]

On D=6D=6 spin representations and supersymmetry:

  • Paul Howe, Germán Sierra, Paul Townsend, Supersymmetry in six dimensions, Nuclear Physics B

    221 2 (1983) 331-348 [doi:10.1016/0550-3213(83)90582-5]

On D=7 supergravity (as gauged supergravity):

  • Paul Townsend, Peter van Nieuwenhuizen, Gauged Seven-Dimensional Supergravity , Phys. Lett. 125B (1983) 41 (spire:189055, doi:10.1016/0370-2693(83)91230-3)

On 3d gravity as a Chern-Simons theory and its holographic relation to a 2d CFT boundary field theory (well before AdS/CFT was conceived from string theory):

  • A. Achucarro, Paul Townsend, A Chern-Simons Action for Three-Dimensional anti-De Sitter Supergravity Theories, Phys. Lett. B180 (1986) 89 (doi:10.1016/0370-2693(86)90140-1, spire:21208)

Understanding extended supersymmetry-algebras as extension by Noether charges of probe brane sigma-models:

  • José de Azcárraga, Jerome Gauntlett, José M. Izquierdo, Paul Townsend: Topological Extensions of the Supersymmetry Algebra for Extended Objects, Phys. Rev. Lett. 63 (1989) 2443 [doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.63.2443, spire:26393]

Understanding super p-brane-species as classified by the Lie algebra cohomology of their target super-Minkowski spacetime super Lie algebras:

  • José de Azcárraga, Paul Townsend: Superspace geometry and the classification of supersymmetric extended objects, Physical Review Letters 62 22 (1989) [doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.62.2579, spire:284635]

Early discussion of the duality between type IIA string theory and M-theory in view of double dimensional reduction of the M2-brane:

  • Paul K. Townsend, The eleven-dimensional supermembrane revisited, Phys. Lett. B 350 (1995) 184-187 [arXiv:hep-th/9501068, doi:10.1016/0370-2693(95)00397-4]

On centrally extended supersymmetry super Lie algebras in string theory, such as the M-theory super Lie algebra:

  • Paul Townsend, pp-Brane Democracy, in: Particles, strings and cosmology Proceedings, 19th Johns Hopkins Workshop and 5th PASCOS Interdisciplinary Symposium, Baltimore, USA, March 22-25 (1995) [arXiv:hep-th/9507048, spire:397058]

    reprinted in: Michael Duff (ed.), The World in Eleven Dimensions, IoP (1999) 375-389

  • Paul Townsend, M(embrane) theory on T 0T^0, Nucl. Phys. Proc. Suppl. 68 (1998) 11-16 [doi:10.1016/S0920-5632(98)00136-4, arXiv:hep-th/9708034]

  • Paul Townsend: M-theory from its superalgebra, in Strings, Branes and Dualities, NATO ASI Series 520, Springer (1999) [arXiv:hep-th/9712004, doi:10.1007/978-94-011-4730-9_5]

On black\,D8-brane-solutions in massive type IIA supergravity/massive type IIA string theory:

  • Eric Bergshoeff, Mees de Roo, Michael Green, George Papadopoulos, Paul Townsend, Duality of Type II 7-branes and 8-branes, Nucl. Phys. B470:113-135, 1996 (arXiv:hep-th/9601150)

On M2-M5-brane bound states:

  • José M. Izquierdo, Neil Lambert, George Papadopoulos, Paul Townsend, Dyonic Membranes, Nucl. Phys. B 460 (1996) 560-578 [arXiv:hep-th/9508177, doi:10.1016/0550-3213(95)00606-0]

  • Michael Green, Neil Lambert, George Papadopoulos, Paul Townsend, Dyonic pp-branes from self-dual (p+1)(p+1)-branes, Phys.Lett.B384:86-92, 1996 (arXiv:hep-th/9605146)

On the 3-brane in 6d:

  • Joaquim Gomis, David Mateos, Joan Simón, Paul Townsend, Brane-Intersection Dynamics from Branes in Brane Backgrounds, Phys. Lett. B430 (1998) 231-236 (arXiv:hep-th/9803040)

On black holes in D=5 supergravity:

  • Jerome Gauntlett, Robert C. Myers, Paul Townsend, Black Holes of D=5D=5 Supergravity, Class.Quant.Grav. 16 (1999) 1-21 [arXiv:hep-th/9810204, doi:10.1088/0264-9381/16/1/001]

On super p-brane sigma models perturbed around the asymptotic boundary of their own black brane near horizon geometry (related to AdS-CFT duality):

  • Piet Claus, Renata Kallosh, Antoine Van Proeyen, M 5-brane and superconformal (0,2)(0,2) tensor multiplet in 6 dimensions, Nucl. Phys. B 518 (1998) 117-150 [arXiv:hep-th/9711161, doi:10.1016/S0550-3213(98)00137-0]

  • Piet Claus, Renata Kallosh, J. Kumar, Paul K. Townsend, Antoine Van Proeyen, Conformal Theory of M2, D3, M5 and “D1+D5” Branes, JHEP 9806 (1998) 004 [arXiv:hep-th/9801206, doi:10.1088/1126-6708/1998/06/004]

On D-brane polarization into supertubes:

  • David Mateos, Paul Townsend, Supertubes, Phys. Rev. Lett. 87 (2001) 011602 (arXiv:hep-th/0103030)

On higher curvature corrections to D=11 supergravity and M-theory on G₂-manifolds:

  • H. Lu, Christopher Pope, Kellogg Stelle, Paul Townsend, Supersymmetric Deformations of G 2G_2 Manifolds from Higher-Order Corrections to String and M-Theory, JHEP 0410:019, 2004 (arXiv:hep-th/0312002)

  • H. Lu, Christopher Pope, Kellogg Stelle, Paul Townsend, String and M-theory Deformations of Manifolds with Special Holonomy, JHEP 0507:075, 2005 (arXiv:hep-th/0410176)

On open M5-branes intersecting M9-branes in a Yang monopole:

  • Eric Bergshoeff, Gary Gibbons, Paul Townsend, Open M5-branes, Phys. Rev. Lett. 97:231601 2006 (arXiv:hep-th/0607193, doi:10.1103/PhysRevLett.97.231601)

On supersymmetry and division algebras, the corresponding twistor space and its AdS version:

  • Alex Arvanitakis, Alec E. Barns-Graham, Paul Townsend, Twistor description of spinning particles in AdS, JHEP 01 (2018) 059 (arXiv:1710.09557)
