Idea

Gromov-Witten theory may be understood as providing a product on formal power series over certain abelian sheaf cohomology groups of Kähler manifolds XX. The quantum cohomology of XX is the resulting Frobenius algebra structure on these formal power series.

Together with Gromov-Witten theory quantum sheaf cohomology was discovered in and has its geometric roots as part of the data that describes certain 2-dimensional sigma-model quantum field theories with target space XX.

Related concepts

  • Quantum cohomology arises as chiral rings in super Yang-Mills theory.
