Idea

There is a close relationship between

  • the four real normed division algebras

  • spin geometry and real (Majorana) spin representations;

  • the Lie algebra cohomology of the super Poincare Lie algebra,

  • and supersymmetry in quantum field theory and string theory.

This is based on the fact that in certain dimensions, spin group representations are naturally identified with a ùïÇ n\mathbb{K}^n, for ùïÇ\mathbb{K} one of the normed division algebras, see at spin group The exceptional isomorphisms.

By the D'Auria-Fre formulation of supergravity the ‚àû-Lie algebra valued forms with values in these constitute the field content of (11-dimensional) supergravity.
