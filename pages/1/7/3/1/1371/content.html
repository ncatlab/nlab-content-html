Idea

As a model for an (‚àû,1)-categories, sSet-enriched categories (‚Äúsimplicial categories‚Äù) may be thought of as a semi-strictification of a quasi-category, where composition along objects is strictly associative and unital.

The equivalence between quasi-categories and sSet-enriched categories as models for (‚àû,1)-categories is exhibited by the operation of sending an sSetsSet-category C\mathbf{C} to the simplicial set N(ùíû)N(\mathcal{C}) called its homotopy-coherent nerve (in generalization of the simplicial nerve of an ordinary category).
