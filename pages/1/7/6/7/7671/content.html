chapter IV: classification of pp divisible groups

Unless otherwise stated let kk be a perfect field of prime characteristic.

We denote write B(K):=Quot(W(k))B(K):=Quot(W(k)) for the quotient field of the Witt ring W(k)W(k).

We extend the Frobenius morphism x↦x (p)x\mapsto x^{(p)} to an automorphism of B(k)B(k). The set of fixed points of x↦x (p)x\mapsto x^{(p)} in W(k)W(k) is W(F p)=ℤ pW(F_p)=\mathbb{Z}_p. The set of fixed points of x↦x (p)x\mapsto x^{(p)} in B(k)B(k) is B(F p)=ℚ pB(F_p)=\mathbb{Q}_p.
