Idea

An ind-object of a category ğ’\mathcal{C} is a formal filtered colimit of objects of ğ’\mathcal{C}. Here â€œformalâ€ means that the colimit is taken in the category of presheaves of ğ’\mathcal{C} (the free cocompletion of ğ’\mathcal{C}). Recalling the nature of filtered colimits, this means that in particular chains of inclusions
