Idea

The specialization of a context of six operations (f *⊣f *)(f^\ast \dashv f_\ast), (f !⊣f !)(f_! \dashv f^!) to the case that the “projection formula” Y⊗f !X≃f !(f *Y⊗X)Y \otimes f_! X \simeq f_!(f^\ast Y \otimes X) holds naturally in X,YX,Y. (May 05, def. 2.12)

Properties

In a Verdier-Grothendieck context, duality intertwines f !f_! with f *f_\ast and f !f^! with f *f^\ast.

(May 05, section 3, Joshua, corollary 5.4)

  • Verdier duality, Grothendieck duality
