Relation to higher prequantum geometry

The 3-form œâ\omega from def. we may regard as equipping ‚Ñù 7\mathbb{R}^7 with 2-plectic structure. From this point of view G 2G_2 is the linear subgroup of the 2-plectomorphism group, hence (up to the translations) the image of the Heisenberg group of (‚Ñù 7,œâ)(\mathbb{R}^7, \omega) in the symplectomorphism group.

Or, dually, we may regard the 4-form ‚ãÜœâ\star \omega of def. as being a 3-plectic structure and G 2G_2 correspondingly as the linear part in the 3-plectomorphism group of ‚Ñù 7\mathbb{R}^7.
