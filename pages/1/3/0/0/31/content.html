I work on mathematical physics — which I interpret broadly as ‘math that could be of interest in physics, and physics that could be of interest in math’. I’m especially interested in nn-categories and their applications. I’ve spent a lot of time explaining these subjects on the web. You can see a sampling below. For a lot more stuff, see my website.

If you visit my personal web here, you’ll see my work with James Dolan on doctrines in algebraic geometry, and the preface of a book I edited with Peter May, called Towards Higher Categories.
