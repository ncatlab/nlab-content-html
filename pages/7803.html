The above formulation is due to Victor Porton. A slight reformulation is that funcoids can be seen as morphisms between certain types of Chu spaces. Todd Trimble has written some notes on funcoids from this point of view here, where they are also shown to be equivalent to topogenous relations provided that the reflexivity condition of the latter is dropped (which is reasonable from an abstract perspective). The category of endofuncoids and continuous functions, and the full subcategory of reflexive endofuncoids, are each topological over SetSet.
