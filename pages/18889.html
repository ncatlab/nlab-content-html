Idea

In perturbative quantum field theory the Gell-Mann-Low renormalization cocycle or ‚Äúrunning of the coupling constants‚Äù describes interaction vertex redefinitions in dependence of scale transformations on spacetime which relate corespondingly rescaled renormalization schemes to each other.

Definition

A priori the St√ºckelberg-Petermann renormalization group is not about scaling transformations. But if scaling transformations happen to produce new S-matrices/renormalization schemes from given ones, then the main theorem of perturbative renormalization induces for each such scaling transformation a re-definition of interaction Lagrangian densities, this is the Gell-Mann-Low renormalization cocycle (Gell-Mann & Low 54, Brunetti-D√ºtsch-Fredenhagen 09) for review see (D√ºtsch 18, section 3.5.3).
