Idea

Khovanov homology is a knot invariant that is a categorification of the Jones polynomial.

Interpretation in QFT

Khovanov homology has long been expected to appear as the observables in a 4-dimensional TQFT in higher analogy of how the Jones polynomial arises as a observables in 3-dimensional Chern-Simons theory.
