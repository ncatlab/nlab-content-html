Idea

The concept of Lawvere distribution is a kind of categorification of the concept of distribution in functional analysis.

To some extent one may think of a sheaf FF on a topological space as being a Set-valued function on that space: to each point xâˆˆXx \in X it assigns the stalk x *FâˆˆSetx^* F \in Set.
