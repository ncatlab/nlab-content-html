Idea

The generalization of the concept of nilpotent Lie algebra from Lie algebras to L-infinity algebras.

Properties

Rational homotopy theory

The central theorem of rational homotopy theory says that the homotopy theory of nilpotent L ∞L_\infty-algebras of finite type over the rational numbers is equivalently that of rational nilpotent topological spaces of finite type.
