Idea

The Seiberg-Witten equations are partial differential equations for connections of principal U(1)-bundles and smooth sections of their spinor bundles. Their moduli space is used to define the Seiberg-Witten invariants capable to study smooth structures on 4-manifolds. Since the gauge group of the Seiberg-Witten equations is abelian, calculations from Donaldson theory could be simplified with new methods provided by the Seiberg-Witten invariants.
