Idea

The recognition principle for finite relative loop spaces states that, for 1≤N<∞1 \leq N \lt \infty, a pair of pointed topolological spaces is of the homotopy type of a relative NN-loop space pair of some (N−1)(N-1)-connected topological pair if and only if it is a grouplike algebra over the Swiss-Cheese relative operad 𝒮𝒞 N\mathcal{SC}_N.

The recognition principle for infinite relative loop spaces states that a pair of pointed toplological spaces is of the homotopy type of a relative ∞\infty-loop space pair of some 1-shifted morphism of connective spectra if and only if it is a grouplike algebra over 𝒮𝒞 ∞\mathcal{SC}_\infty.

Weak Quillen quasiadjunction

The following definition introduced in [Vi20] is a generalization of Quillen adjunctions between model categories. The basic idea is that to construct the unit and counit natural transformations of an adjuction between the homotopy categories it suffices to construct a unit natural span and counit natural cospan at the model categories level, plus some additional compatibility conditions.

Definition

Let 𝒯\mathcal{T} and 𝒜\mathcal{A} be model categories. A weak Quillen quasiadjunction, or just quasiadjunction, between 𝒯\mathcal{T} and 𝒜\mathcal{A}, denoted by

(S⊣ C,FΛ):𝒯⇌𝒜, (S \dashv_{\ C,F}\Lambda):\mathcal{T}\rightleftharpoons \mathcal{A},

is a quadruple of functors

with SS the left quasiadjoint and Λ\Lambda the right quasiadjoint, equipped with a natural span in 𝒯\mathcal{T} and a natural cospan in 𝒜\mathcal{A}

such that

(i) SS is left derivable;

(ii) Λ\Lambda is right derivable;

(iii) 𝒞\mathscr{C} and ℱ\mathscr{F} preserve cofibrant and fibrant objects;

(iv) η′\eta' and ϵ′\epsilon' are natural weak equivalences;

(v) If X∈𝒯X\in\mathcal{T} is cofibrant then ϵ SXSη X≃ϵ′ SXSη X′\epsilon_{SX}S\eta_X\simeq \epsilon'_{SX}S\eta_X';

(vi) If Y∈𝒜Y\in\mathcal{A} is fibrant then Λϵ Yη ΛY≃Λϵ′ Yη′ ΛY\Lambda\epsilon_Y \eta_{\Lambda Y}\simeq \Lambda\epsilon'_Y\eta'_{\Lambda Y}.

\begin{theorem}[{\citeTheorem 2.1.2{Vi20}}] A quasiadjunction induces an adjunction

(𝕃S⊣ℝΛ):ℋo𝒯⇌ℋo𝒜 (\mathbb{L}S \dashv\mathbb{R}\Lambda):\mathcal{H}o \mathcal{T}\rightleftharpoons \mathcal{H}o \mathcal{A}

between the homotopy categories. \end{theorem}

Finite relative recognition theorem

Let 1≤N≤∞1\leq N\leq \infty and 𝒮𝒞 N\mathcal{SC}_N be the cubical Swiss Cheese relative operad, a colored operad over the colors {c,o}\{c, o\}, respectively referred to as the closed and open colors. It induces a monad SC NSC_N on the category Top * 2Top_*^2 of pairs of pointed topological spaces. Denote by 𝒮𝒞 N[Top]\mathcal{SC}_N[Top] the category of algebras over SC NSC_N, which we equip with the mixed model structure induced by the one on Top * 2Top_*^2. We denote pairs of pointed spaces as X=(X c,X o)X=(X_{c},X_{o}).

Denote by Top * →Top_*^\rightarrow the category of pointed maps, equipped with the mixed projective model structure. We denote maps as Y=(Y:Y 0→Y 1)Y=(Y:Y_0\rightarrow Y_1).
