Idea

The generalization of the bicategory Span to (‚àû,n)-categories:

An (‚àû,n)(\infty,n)-category of correspondences in ‚àû-groupoid is an (‚àû,n)-category whose

  • objects are ‚àû-groupoids;

  • morphisms X‚ÜíYX \to Y are correspondences

Definition via coalgebras

In (BenZvi-Nadler 13, remark 1.17) it is observed that

Corr n(H)‚âÉE nAlg b(H op) Corr_n(\mathbf{H}) \simeq E_n Alg_b(\mathbf{H}^{op})

(This immediately implies that every object in Corr n(H)Corr_n(\mathbf{H}) is a self-fully dualizable object.)

To see how this works, consider X‚ààHX \in \mathbf{H} any object regarded as a coalgebra in H\mathbf{H} via its diagonal map (here). Then a comodule EE over it is a co-action

Then for
