Differential algebraic K-theory is the differential cohomology-refinement of algebraic K-theory.

In (Bunke-Tamme 12) this is realized effectively as the differential cohomology in a cohesive topos of the tangent (‚àû,1)-topos of the cohesive (‚àû,1)-topos
