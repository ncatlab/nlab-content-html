References

A general account of the structure is in

  • Olivera Miskovic, Ricardo Troncoso, Jorge Zanelli, Canonical sectors of five-dimensional Chern-Simons theories (arXiv:hep-th/0504055)

Remarks on applications in phenomemenological models are in

  • C. Hill, Physics of D=5D = 5 Chern-Simons term (pdf)

The relation to 5-dimensional supergravity is discussed in

  • A. Ceresole, A. Lerda, and P. van Nieuwenhuizen , Five-dimensional gravitational super Chern-Simons terms, Phys. Rev. D 34, 1744–1748 (1986)

  • Yuji Tachikawa, Five-dimensional Chern-Simons terms and Nekrasov’s instanton counting (arXiv:hep-th/0401184)

and via KK-compactification of the supergravity C-field of 11-dimensional supergravity in

  • Federico Bonetti, Thomas Grimm, Stefan Hohenegger, One-loop Chern-Simons terms in five dimensions (arXiv:1302.2918)

More on supersymmetric 5d CS theory is in

  • Sergei M. Kuzenko, Joseph Novak, On supersymmetric Chern-Simons-type theories in five dimensions, JHEP 1402 (2014) 096 (arXiv:1309.6803)
